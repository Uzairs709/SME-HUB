Introduction

"Small and Medium Enterprises HUB is an autonomous institution of the Government of Pakistan under Ministry of Industries and Production. SME HUB was established in October 1998 for encouraging and facilitating the development and growth of small and medium enterprises in the country. SME HUB is not only an SME policy-advisory body for the government of Pakistan but also facilitates other stakeholders in addressing their SME development agendas."
